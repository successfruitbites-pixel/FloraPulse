Apple Health

Manual

Export your data to import into Apple Health.

Note: Apple Health only supports direct syncing from native iOS apps. As a web app, FloraPulse provides a CSV export that you can use for your records or manual entry.

How to import manually:

  1. Tap Export CSV below and save the file to your iPhone.
  2. Open the Health app on your iPhone.
  3. Tap Browse > Cycle Tracking.
  4. Tap Add Data in the top right corner.
  5. Use your exported CSV as a reference to log your dates.
