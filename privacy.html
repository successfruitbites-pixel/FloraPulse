Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Your Data Stays on Your Device

At FloraPulse, we believe your health data is deeply personal. That's why we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal health information to our servers.

How Data is Stored

All information you enter into FloraPulse—including your last period date, cycle length, and personal notes—is saved exclusively in your browser's local storage (localStorage). This means your data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising scripts that could monitor your usage or access your health data.

Clearing Your Data

Because your data is stored locally, you have complete control over it. You can delete all your FloraPulse data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or cache.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how we handle data (for example, if we introduce an optional cloud sync feature in the future), we will update this policy and ask for your explicit consent before any data leaves your device.

This tool is for planning purposes only and is not a substitute for medical advice.
